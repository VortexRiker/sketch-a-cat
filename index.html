Sketch-A-Cat

Welcome to a digital canvas of Boris!

A place where the most beautiful pictures of cats come to live in all colors! Or maybe not even cats? All kinds of creatures are welcome to be depicted on this canvas!

Have fun and enjoy your creations!

Instructions

  • To draw press and hold LMB in the canvas area
  • To clear the canvas press Reset Button
  • To change canvas resolution (number of cells representing the canvas) press Configure Button. Prompt will ask you to enter a one dimension of canvas resolution i.e. number of cells in a row/column (doesn't matter row or column since canvas is a square)
  • To show/hide canvas grid press Grid Button

Additional information

  • Color of the brush is selected randomly on each new LMB press
  • Leaving the canvas area will deselect the brush if one is currently active
